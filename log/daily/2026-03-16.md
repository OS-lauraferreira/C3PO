## Context: Jira Workflow Optimization Documentation

**Objective:** Optimize internal team work management by documenting the Jira workflow process.

**Workflow Process:**
1. **Define & Test Specifications:** New implementations are first defined and tested on the **RFC Demo Board**
2. **Validate in Demo:** Specifications and automations are validated in the Demo environment
3. **Team Alignment:** Specifications must be aligned with respective teams before entering production
4. **Roll-out to Production:** After validation and team alignment, implementations are rolled out to the **RFC Production Board** (where teams execute actual work)
5. **Team Communication:** Communicate changes to teams via Slack channels:
   - `#odc_cab_approvals` — CAB approvals and governance updates
   - `#odc-practices-feedback` — Process feedback and optimization discussions

**Critical Constraints:**
- **Avoid Friday Deployments:** Changes should NOT be implemented in production on Fridays to ensure no errors occur during the weekend
- **Preferred Implementation Window:** Deploy changes during the week (Monday–Thursday) to allow proper support and monitoring
- **Team Alignment Required:** All specifications must be validated with relevant teams before production deployment
