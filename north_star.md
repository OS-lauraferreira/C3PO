## Strategic Themes

### This Year: ### Method 2: Transform Change Management

**Goal:** Minimize production risk while maximizing developer throughput by automating gates and centralizing artifacts.

**M2.1: Ensure Pipeline Integrity by Consolidating Artifact Centralization**
- Unify the Change Catalog and enforce CI/CD compliance prerequisites to eliminate "shadow" production changes
- Output: Centralized Change Catalog (Normal & Standard Changes), Out of catalog RFC Process definition
- Jira: RSA-753 | Timeline: Q1 2026 | Effort: 8

**M2.2: Streamline Approval Workflows by Establishing Reviewer Empowerment**
- Deploy a framework that empowers CAB to reject bypasses and streamlines approvals through clearly defined authority
- Output: Framework definition, Training Plan for Reviewers, Monitoring
- Jira: RSA-813 | Timeline: Q1 2026 | Effort: 5

**M2.3: Optimize the Automation Pipeline by Executing Discovery Rituals & Formalizing Standard Promotion**
- Audit manual logs monthly to identify high-impact automation candidates for the 2027 technical roadmap and codify formal governance to promote high-success Normal Changes to the pre-approved Standard track
- Output: Process definition
- Jira: RSA-775 | Timeline: Q2-Q3 2026 | Effort: 8

**M2.4: Enable Trusted Governance by Correlating CFR to Asset-Version Changes**
- Mature the Change Failure Rate (CFR) metric by linking failures directly to specific asset versions
- Output: Define RFC↔Failure correlation
- Jira: RSA-812 | Timeline: Q2-Q3 2026 | Effort: 5

**M2.5: Harden Data Reliability by Building CFR Technical Implementation**
- Build the automated data science logic to enable Asset-to-Incident correlation
- Output: Dashboard, CFR updated
- Jira: RSA-856 | Timeline: Q3 2026 | Effort: 5

**M2.6: Accelerate Delivery Velocity by Automating Lifecycles**
- Trigger automated change closure
- Output: Control Mechanism defined, Dashboard changes
- Jira: RSA-857 | Timeline: Q3 2026 | Effort: 5

**M2.7: Bridge Competency Gaps by Delivering Specialized Change Training**
- Develop and deliver specialized training tracks for Change interveners to improve RFC quality and compliance
- Output: Training plan
- Jira: RSA-858 | Timeline: Q4 2026 | Effort: 5

**M2.8: Guarantee Change Audit Readiness by Verifying Control Monitoring**
- Conduct regular dry-runs and validate technical evidence for Change Management controls (SOC2/ISO/FedRAMP)
- Output: Evidence ready, Regular process audit confirmed
