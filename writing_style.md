## Words and Phrases to AVOID

*These make writing sound like AI, not like you:*

### Generic AI-Sounding Words
- "Delve" / "delve into"
- "Utilize" (just say "use")
- "Leverage" as a verb (unless genuinely appropriate)
- "Synergy" / "synergize"
- "Circle back"
- "Touch base"
- "Move the needle"
- "At the end of the day"
- "It goes without saying"
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "Per my last email"
- "Going forward"
- "Actionable insights"
- "Paradigm shift"
- "Low-hanging fruit"
- "Deep dive"
- "Unpack"
- "Holistic"
- "Robust"
- "Seamless"

### Phrases That Sound Fake
- "I'm thrilled to..."
- "I'm excited to share..."
- "I wanted to reach out..."
- "Just following up..."
- "Happy to help!"
- "Great question!"
- "Absolutely!"
- "Thanks for your patience"
- "Please don't hesitate to..."
